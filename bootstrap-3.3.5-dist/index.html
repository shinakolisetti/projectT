Welcome to Rutgers Tennis Club Site

Fall Semester 2015

Rutgers Tennis Club is a co-ed tennis club that allows students with high school and club experience to continue playing tennis at a competitive level.

The club is comprised of the traveling team that competes with other universities at tournaments throughout the country and the recreational team that less competitive and more for personal improvement and fun.

Everyone is welcome to join! We encourage all Rutgers students, staff, and faculty to join us for another exciting semester. Participating in club tennis is a fun, exciting, and unique college experience. So invite your friends, grab your racquets, and head out to the courts! Also check out the contact info section, to find out more information.

Please regularly check the site for updates!
